Music Department

Worship that equips and sends.

The Discipleship Mission Music Department uses God-honouring worship and original music to strengthen the local church, deepen discipleship, and carry the Gospel into communities that words alone cannot reach.

Join the team → What we do
Our vision

Music rooted in the Word, offered to God, and shared with the world.

We believe that music and worship are not decorations to a church service — they are vehicles for teaching, discipleship, lament, and praise. Every song we produce and every team we train is built on that conviction.

Our Music Department works alongside the training programme to create worship that reinforces Scripture, builds up the saints, and draws people who would not otherwise step into a church building.

What we value

The pillars of our worship culture

✝️

Christ-Centred

Every lyric and melody must exalt Jesus, not the performer or the style.

📖

Biblically Grounded

Songs are tested against Scripture before they are sung in any of our churches.

🤲

Servant-Hearted

The worship team leads from humility, preparing hearts to hear God's Word.

🌍

Mission-Minded

We write and translate worship in local languages to reach every community.

🎓

Equipping Others

We train worship leaders in small churches who lack resources for music school.

🤝

Unified in Purpose

Music and teaching walk hand in hand — the song prepares the soil; the Word plants the seed.

Ministry areas

Four ways music serves the mission

🎙️

Congregational Worship Training

Equipping church worship teams with skills, song selection, and devotional depth to lead well every Sunday.

🎵

Original Music & Recording

Writing and recording original worship songs rooted in Scripture — available free of charge to partner churches.

🌐

Language & Translation Ministry

Translating key worship songs into Swahili, French, and local African languages so every church can sing in its heart language.

📺

Music on DM TV

Live worship sessions and teaching on music ministry available through the Discipleship Mission TV platform.
