Discipleship Training Program

What we teach: four modules from the Word

A complete pathway, designed to be walked through with a mentor — from first believing the Gospel to being sent out to make disciples of others.

Module 1

Following Christ

  • The Gospel
  • Repentance and Faith
  • Assurance of Salvation
  • Abiding in the Word
  • Prayer
  • Bible Study
Module 2

Loving God

  • The Attributes of God
  • Delighting in God
  • Obeying Jesus
  • Overcoming Temptation
  • Walking by the Spirit and in the Light
Module 3

Cultivating Godliness

  • Practicing the One-Anothers
  • Committing to the Local Church
  • The Use of Time
  • Money and Possessions
  • Humility
  • Forgiveness
Module 4

Making Disciples

  • Fasting
  • Spiritual Gifts
  • Evangelism
  • The Cost of Discipleship
  • Disciples Making Disciples
  • Catching a World Vision
How a church runs it

Simple enough for a volunteer leader to mentor

1

Pair a mentor

Each believer is paired with a trained mentor or small group for weekly study.

2

Walk the modules

Work through Modules 1–4 in order, at a pace that fits the local church's rhythm.

3

Deploy as disciplers

Graduates are commissioned to mentor the next believer — the cycle continues.
