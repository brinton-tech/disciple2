The people behind the mission

Discipleship Mission is led by a team of servants — founders, directors, coordinators, and partners — each carrying a piece of the vision to equip small churches and send disciples to grow.

Founding Leadership
Pastor & Co-Founder

Nzanzu Muviswa Aggelos

Executive Director

Pastor Aggelos is the visionary co-founder of Discipleship Mission. He holds an undergraduate degree in Theology from Africa International University, Nairobi, and is currently pursuing a Master's in Biblical Studies. Married to Dorcas Kalepa, father of ElSali.

My heart beats for Christ, for His Church, and for every soul He calls us to disciple.
Pastor & Co-Founder

Katembo Emery Kuhimba

Ministry Director

Pastor Emery co-founded Discipleship Mission with a heart for seeing small churches transformed. He holds a Diploma from Pwani Bible College, Mombasa. Married to Dorcas Kavira, father of Louange and Abdias.

We live to honor Christ and build His Kingdom together as one family in ministry.
