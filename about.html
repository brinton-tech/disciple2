About us

Called to serve. Committed to disciple. Sent to grow.

Discipleship Mission is a Christian ministry dedicated to strengthening small and growing churches through biblical discipleship, leadership development, and church health training.

Who we are

Bridging the gap with practical, affordable training

Many churches desire to grow but lack access to theological education, discipleship materials, and leadership mentoring. We exist to bridge that gap by providing practical and affordable training rooted in Scripture.

Our ministry serves pastors, church elders, ministry leaders, youth leaders, church planters, and believers who desire to grow in their faith and ministry effectiveness.

Statement of faith

What we believe

The Bible is the inspired and authoritative Word of God.

There is one God eternally existing as Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

Jesus Christ is fully God and fully man.

Salvation is by grace through faith in Jesus Christ alone.

The Holy Spirit empowers believers for holy living and ministry.

The Church is God's instrument for making disciples of all nations.

Jesus Christ will return in power and glory.
