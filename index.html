Our vision & mission

Healthy churches that make disciples who make disciples

We believe that when church leaders are properly trained and disciples are grounded in God's Word, churches grow both spiritually and numerically.

  • Vision — To see healthy, disciple-making churches transforming communities throughout Africa and the world.
  • Mission — To equip pastors, church leaders, and believers with sound biblical teaching, discipleship resources, and leadership development.
How we equip a church

Disciple. Develop. Deploy.

Our entire training program moves believers through one clear path — from following Christ to being sent out to make disciples of others.

1

Disciple

Believers are grounded in the Gospel, prayer, and the Word through guided study with a mentor.

2

Develop

Leaders are trained in godly character, church commitment, and practical ministry skill.

3

Deploy

Trained disciples are sent to make disciples of others — multiplying healthy churches.

Discipleship Training Program

Four modules, rooted in Scripture

Module 1

Following Christ

The Gospel, repentance and faith, assurance of salvation, prayer and Bible study.

Module 2

Loving God

The attributes of God, obeying Jesus, overcoming temptation, walking by the Spirit.

Module 3 & 4

Cultivating Godliness & Making Disciples

Local church commitment, evangelism, the cost of discipleship, and catching a world vision.
